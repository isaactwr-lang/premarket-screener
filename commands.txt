PREMARKET SCREENER — TERMINAL COMMANDS
================================

--- SCHEDULING (run once to activate) ---

Install the schedule:
  cp "/Users/isaacteo/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/Resume Stuff/Coding Portfolio/Trading Bot/com.tradingbot.gappers.plist" ~/Library/LaunchAgents/
  launchctl load ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.tradingbot.gappers.plist

Confirm it's active:
  launchctl list | grep tradingbot

Stop the schedule:
  launchctl unload ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.tradingbot.gappers.plist


--- RUNNING MANUALLY ---

Run the bot now:
  cd "/Users/isaacteo/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/Resume Stuff/Coding Portfolio/Trading Bot"
  python main.py

View today's log:
  cat "/Users/isaacteo/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/Resume Stuff/Coding Portfolio/Trading Bot/logs/run_$(date +%Y-%m-%d).log"

Fires: every day at 8:30pm GMT+8 (= 8:30am US Eastern Time, deep in premarket)

Logs:  Trading Bot/logs/run_YYYY-MM-DD.log
